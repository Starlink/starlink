SLITIM

Usage

SLITIM=_CHAR

Description

The frame to be used for determining the limits of the dekker.

This should be a frame which clearly delineates the inter-order gap and the full extent of the slit. In general the arc-lamp frame is used, but a flat-field frame may also work.
